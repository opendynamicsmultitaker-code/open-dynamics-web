CORE FEATURES

One-Click Sample Guide (REAL-TIME)

The world's first Sample Guide that organizes your audio visually in Real-Time using our "Flight Lanes" algorithm. No more guessing. See your sound, avoid collisions, and mix visually.

Post-Awareness Scrambling

An advanced stochastic sequencer. Create complex time-traveling structures, micro-loops, and pitch variations that degrade over time. Not just a glitch effect, but a memory loss simulator.

Slightly Scrambled

For subtle degradation. A dedicated buffer engine that introduces minor temporal inconsistencies and jitters, perfect for that "barely holding together" tape feeling.

Layer Mixer System

Combine multiple .taker projects into a massive .tlayer soundscape. Automate fade-ins, fade-outs, and create evolving albums with a single tool.

RAM-First Architecture

Audio is loaded fully into memory allowing for instant Varispeed, Reverse, and Granular Tone shifting with zero disk latency.

"The Bounce" Recording

Capture your performance live. Every knob movement, every scramble, and every imperfection is recorded to WAV or OGG as you hear it.
