MULTI-TAKER™

The professional deconstruction and atmosphere engine.

DEMONSTRATION

Promotional Video Coming Soon

Multi-Taker is a standalone audio processing environment designed for creating ethereal atmospheres, loop imperfections, and time-based deconstruction. It operates entirely in RAM for zero-latency playback manipulation and features a modular rack of high-fidelity effects.

CORE FEATURES

RAM-First Architecture

Audio is loaded fully into memory for instant Reverse, Varispeed, and Granular Tone shifting without disk latency.

Modular Effect Rack

Chain effects dynamically. Includes Modulated FDN Reverb, Offset Delay, TPT EQ, and more.

Real-Time Recording

"The Bounce" engine captures your performance and knob movements live to WAV or OGG.

Sample-Accurate Loops

Custom looping engine with millisecond-precision Crossfade (X-Fade) to eliminate clicks.

Glassmorphism UI

A modern, fluid interface designed for focus and aesthetic pleasure.

Spectral Drone

FFT-based accumulator that freezes audio into infinite harmonic pads (Studio/Pro only).
